    Frequently Asked Questions

    The last day for videos submission is 30th of September 2024, and 8th October 2024 for late submissions, for which there is an extra fee of $5 being a total registration fee of $10.

    Dance Techniques, Expressions, Choreography and Performance Quality are the judging criteria for each and every dance presented either solo or team.

    The winner shall receive a proportion of 10% cash prize from the collected registration fees, a mystery gift alongside the winning international certificate from the competition. The second and third positions shall also receive winning international certificates. All the participants shall receive an international certificate for participation.

    The competition is both for solo as well as team performers. There is no limit for team performers, however, we do not expect more than 15 performers dancing as a team.

    The dance is not limited to a particular category and can range from classical to hip-hop. However, the competition does encourage indigenous or cultural dances. The dance has to be a minimum of a minute and maximum of 5 minutes long.

    ANYONE and EVERYONE between the ages 5-19 who loves dancing and is willing to be a part of the competition is MORE THAN WELCOME to take part.

    For national level round, the judges will judge the dance privately, and for the international and the continental round, the dances will be judged live on Youtube.

    There shall be feedbacks on the individual performances if they enter the International Round, however for the individual feedbacks on participants that did not reach the International Round, you can receive feedbacks by contacting narueuchiha1@gmail.com for where payment shall be necessary.

    Please fill the form for the competition and we shall update you through your mail with the recent news of the competition.
